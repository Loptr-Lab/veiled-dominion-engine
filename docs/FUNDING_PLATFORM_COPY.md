## Public Tier Framing Notes

For public funding platforms, keep tier promises simple:
- optional recognition
- general supporter updates
- early visibility into roadmap framing where possible
- no hard SLA or guaranteed consulting language on public casual-support pages

Use direct contact for larger sponsorship, procurement, or invoicing requests.
