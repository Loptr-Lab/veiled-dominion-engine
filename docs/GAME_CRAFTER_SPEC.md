# VEILED DOMINION — GAME CRAFTER SPECIFICATION SHEET

> **Target Aesthetic:** Brutalist Tech-Noir. Flat, heavy fills, absolute high-contrast overlays, and clinical geometric framing. 
> **Core Goal:** Physically manifest the psychological weight of "restraint" on a custom tabletop layout.

## EXECUTIVE ARTIFACTS
*   **Primary Palette:**
    *   Deep Obsidian Black (`#0D0C0E`)
    *   Brutalist Concrete Charcoal (`#1A191C`)
    *   Blood Crimson (`#9E1B1B`)
    *   Sterile Gold (`#D4AF37`)
    *   Industrial White (`#F5F5F7`)

---

## 1. BOARD & PRINTED MATERIALS

### Custom Cross-Shaped Board
*   **TGC Component:** Custom Large Quad-Fold Board (or custom laser-cut tile set).
*   **Dimensions:** 14x14 grid total, featuring a neutral 2x2 center.
*   **Graphic Specifications:**
    *   High-contrast black grids with thin, hairline silver rules.
    *   Grid quadrants colored to represent starting zones: three standard dark grey/charcoal zones for Mortal Factions, one stark Crimson/Translucent red zone for the Rebirth Faction.
    *   Apply a 45-degree diagonal hatch pattern overlay to denote boundary squares of the neutral zone (mirroring the digital "Veil State" pattern).
    *   **Finish:** Matte UV coating to limit glare, maintaining a clean, technical presentation.

### Rulebook Booklet
*   **TGC Component:** 8-Page Booklet (Medium).
*   **Paper Type:** Heavy 80lb text paper with a semi-gloss synthetic cover.
*   **Layout Rules:**
    *   Strict center-cut Y-axis split components: left-hand pages feature clinical vector schematics of piece movements; right-hand pages show gritty high-contrast ink illustrations of cosmic lore.
    *   All tables and call-outs must have a left-accented solid border in Blood Crimson (`#9E1B1B`).

## 2. GAME PIECES & TOKEN SPECIFICATIONS

> **Visual Contrast Spec:** 
> `DEATH (Void): Matte Black / Light-Absorbing` **VS** `REBIRTH (Spark): Amber Resin / Light-Diffusing`

### The Faction Armies

#### Three Mortal Armies
*   **TGC Component:** Standard Chess Pieces or Custom Laser-Cut Acrylic Standees.
*   **Aesthetics:**
    *   **Army 1:** Matte White.
    *   **Army 2:** Slate/Matte Grey.
    *   **Army 3:** Matte Black.
*   **Composition per Army:** 1 Leader (King), 1 Queen, 2 Rooks, 2 Bishops, 2 Knights, 8 Pawns.

#### The Rebirth Army
*   **TGC Component:** Custom Laser-Cut Acrylic or 3D-Printed Figurines.
*   **Aesthetics:**
    *   **Death (The Mentor):** A massive, perfectly smooth sphere or heavy geometric block. Painted in the deepest, ultra-matte light-absorbing black available to emulate a void.
    *   **Rebirth (The Daughter):** A perfect sphere or humanoid figure printed in high-gloss, crystal-clear amber translucent resin.
    *   **The Rest of the Army:** Standard black-coated components for 1 Leader, 1 Rook, 2 Bishops, 2 Knights, and 8 Pawns.

### Trackers & Tokens

#### The Aura Ring (Radius of Ruin)
*   **TGC Component:** Custom Large Circular Acrylic Ring (Translucent Orange or Frosted Red).
*   **Specifications:** Sized to sit exactly over a single board square and drape over the 8 immediately surrounding squares, giving physical presence to the "Radius of Ruin".

#### Boon Tokens
*   **TGC Component:** 20mm Custom Circle Fiber Board Chits (5 per player).
*   **Graphic Specifications:** High-contrast solid Crimson backing (`#9E1B1B`) featuring a clean, minimalist geometric glyph.

#### Leadership Point (LP) Tracker
*   **TGC Component:** Dual-dial chipboard tracker OR a scoring track printed directly onto the board's edge.
*   **Specifications:** Increments from 0 to 10 LP to monitor the Rebirth player's progress toward a Mercy Victory.

## 3. LEGACY / VARIANT COMPONENT: THE 52 CARDS OF WAR

> *Include this deck if manufacturing or playtesting the Stage 2 hybrid card-and-dice mechanics.*

*   **TGC Component:** Poker Deck (52 cards on 11.6pt Premium Linen cardstock).
*   **Visual Design:** Stark, clinical card faces displaying exact values and mechanical powers with minimal ornamentation.
*   **Back Cover Design:** Monochromatic black tile pattern with a single gold-leaf line cutting down the middle.

### The Core Suits
*   **Spades (Conquest):** Pure Crimson text (`#9E1B1B`). Direct attacks (*Skirmish Strike, War March, Siege Blade*).
*   **Hearts (Restoration):** Muted Gold accents (`#D4AF37`). Recovery actions (*Ember Spark, Healer's Touch, Phoenix Rite*).
*   **Clubs (Fortification):** Dark Concrete Grey backgrounds (`#1A191C`). Shielding tactics (*Wicker Shield, Fortress Gate, Unbreakable Line*).
*   **Diamonds (Intelligence):** Stark Industrial White highlights (`#F5F5F7`). Subterfuge mechanics (*Whisper Network, Scout's Report, Grand Betrayal*).

## 4. PACKAGING & BOX DESIGN

*   **TGC Component:** Large Stout Box (Brutalist matte-finish black cardboard).
*   **Box Top Graphic:** High-contrast, full-bleed imagery of the translucent glowing core of the Rebirth piece casting light across an unreflective Musou Black void.
*   **Interior Insert:** Custom vacuum-formed black plastic tray designed to secure the obsidian-style board, the fragile translucent resin pieces, and the card deck safely.
